About This Project

This project focuses on detecting objects such as vehicles and people in aerial drone imagery, addressing challenges like varying lighting, overlapping objects, and complex backgrounds. By leveraging deep learning, it enhances object detection reliability, with applications in smart city planning, traffic management, and surveillance.
